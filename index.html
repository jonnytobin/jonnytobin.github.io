The Cliffs of Moher are one of the top tourist destinations in Ireland. Located on the west coast of the country in County Clare they stand over 200 meters tall and provide breathtaking views of the surrounding islands.

Malin Head

Malin Head is on the Inishowen Peninsula in County Donegal, Ireland and is the most northerly point of the island of Ireland. It is the first stop on the first main attraction on the Wild Atlantic Way.
